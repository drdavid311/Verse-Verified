Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Verse & Verified ("the app," "we," "us") is published by Thirty2Give. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and your choices. We built Verse & Verified to work with as little data collection as possible.

Information We Collect

Verse & Verified does not require you to create an account, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party.

  • No account or sign-in. The app does not ask for your name, email, or any identifying information.
  • No analytics or tracking. The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services, and does not track your activity across other apps or websites.
  • Saved content stays on your device. When you bookmark a story or search, that data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to it, and it is not transmitted to any server we control.

Scripture Searches and Story Results

When you enter a scripture reference, the app processes that request to return relevant news and historical stories. This request is not linked to any personal identifier and is not stored or associated with you individually.

Third-Party Links

Stories in the app link out to their original news or historical sources. Once you tap "Read full story" and leave the app, you are subject to that website's own privacy policy, which we do not control.

Children's Privacy

Verse & Verified does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes — for example, if we later add optional accounts or analytics — we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app can be sent to david@thirty2give.com.

The Scripture

John 3:16

John 3:16 captures the essence of divine love that expresses itself through self-sacrifice to secure the life and liberation of others. In the context of African American history, this theme of sacrificial love is reflected in the lives of those who have risked their own safety, endured suffering, and persevered through profound injustice to secure freedom, dignity, and a better future for their community and for humanity at large.
